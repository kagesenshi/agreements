These Business Development Service Terms ("Business Development Terms") establish the framework under which the party designated as the referrer ("Referrer") and the party designated as the recipient ("Recipient") (collectively referred to as the "Parties" and individually as a "Party"), shall cooperate to identify and refer potential business opportunities. These Business Development Terms are incorporated by reference into the primary agreement between the Parties or any Referral Registration executed hereunder.

A. **DEFINITIONS AND INTERPRETATION**

   1. In this Agreement, the following words have the meanings given below:

      a. **"Prospective Client"** means a person, company, or entity introduced by the Referrer to the Recipient who has not been in negotiation, contract, or communication with the Recipient within the six (6) months prior to the date of Introduction.  

      b. **"Introduction"** means the provision of the Prospective Client’s contact details and a facilitated meeting or correspondence between the Recipient and the Prospective Client.  

      c. **"Successful Contract"** means a binding written agreement, purchase order, or contract entered into between the Recipient and a Prospective Client within the Validity Period.  

      d. **"Gross Revenue"** means the actual gross monetary amount received by the Recipient from the Prospective Client, excluding any (i) sales, service, or value-added taxes; (ii) shipping, insurance, or freight charges; (iii) refunds, credits, or rebates actually granted to the Prospective Client; and (iv) any pass-through costs or third-party license fees specifically identified in the Successful Contract.

      e. **"Business Development Fee"** means the commission payable to the Referrer as set out in **Schedule A** or the applicable Referral Registration.

B. **QUALIFICATION AND ELIGIBILITY**

   1. **Definition of Qualified Lead:** A "Qualified Lead" is a Prospective Client that meets the following minimum criteria at the time of Introduction:
      a. **Need:** The Prospective Client has a demonstrable need for the Recipient's services or products.
      b. **Authority:** The contact provided is a decision-maker or has significant influence over procurement.
      c. **Budget:** There is a reasonable expectation of budget availability for the proposed engagement.
      d. **No Conflict:** The Prospective Client is not an existing customer, partner, or in active negotiation with the Recipient.

   2. **Right of Rejection:** The Recipient reserves the right to reject any Introduction that does not meet the criteria of a Qualified Lead or is otherwise incompatible with the Recipient’s strategic interests. The Recipient shall notify the Referrer of any rejection within five (5) business days of the Introduction.

   3. **Protection for Rejected Leads:** If the Recipient rejects an Introduction, the Recipient agrees not to enter into any business relationship with the rejected Prospective Client for a period of **twelve (12) months** following the date of rejection. 

   4. **Fee Trigger for Rejected Leads:** Notwithstanding Section B.3, if the Recipient conducts business with a rejected Prospective Client within the twelve (12) month period, such business shall be deemed a "Successful Contract" under these terms, and the Business Development Fee shall be immediately payable on all resulting Gross Revenue.

   5. **Abuse Prevention:** If the Referrer repeatedly submits leads that fail to meet the qualification criteria, the Recipient may, in its sole discretion, terminate these Business Development Terms or the primary agreement immediately upon written notice.

C. **APPOINTMENT AND SCOPE**

   1. **Non-Exclusive Appointment:** The Recipient appoints the Referrer on a non-exclusive basis to identify and refer Qualified Leads. The Recipient reserves the right to engage other referrers or market directly to clients. Similarly, the Referrer reserves the right to introduce prospective clients to third parties and is not restricted to referring business solely to the Recipient.

   2. **Process:** To claim an Introduction, the Referrer must notify the Recipient in writing (via email or official letter) identifying the Prospective Client and opportunity. The Recipient must acknowledge receipt and confirm if the lead is valid and constitutes a Qualified Lead (within 5 business days).

D. **COMMISSION AND PAYMENT TERMS**

   1. **Fee Calculation:** The Recipient agrees to pay the Referrer the Business Development Fee calculated as a percentage of the Gross Revenue generated from the Successful Contract, as detailed in **Schedule A**. 

   2. **Pay-When-Paid:** The Business Development Fee becomes payable only after the Recipient has received cleared funds from the Prospective Client. 

   3. **Payment Timeline:** The Recipient shall pay the Business Development Fee within **thirty (30) days** of receiving payment from the Prospective Client. 

   4. **Currency:** All payments shall be made in **Ringgit Malaysia (MYR)** via bank transfer to the account nominated by the Referrer. 

   5. **Taxes:** The Business Development Fee is exclusive of any Service Tax. 

   6. **Variation of Rates:** Notwithstanding Clause 1., the Parties may mutually agree in writing (including via email or official correspondence) to apply a different commission rate for specific opportunities or Prospective Clients. In such instances, the specifically agreed rate shall supersede the default rate set out in Schedule A for that particular Introduction or opportunities.

E. **AUDIT RIGHTS**

   1. **Record Keeping:** The Recipient shall maintain accurate books and records related to any Successful Contracts and Gross Revenue for at least two (2) years following the termination of these terms.

   2. **Audit:** Upon reasonable written notice, the Referrer (or its authorized representative) shall have the right to audit the Recipient's records solely to verify the accuracy of the Business Development Fees paid. Such audit shall be conducted during normal business hours and no more than once per calendar year.

   3. **Costs:** The Referrer shall bear the cost of the audit unless the audit reveals an underpayment of more than five percent (5%), in which case the Recipient shall reimburse the Referrer for the reasonable costs of the audit.

F. **TERM AND TERMINATION**

   1. **Term:** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period defined in the primary agreement, unless terminated earlier. 

   2. **Termination:** Either Party may terminate this Agreement by giving **thirty (30) days'** written notice to the other Party. 

   3. **Post-Termination Rights:** Termination shall not affect the Referrer’s right to receive fees for any Successful Contracts entered into prior to the date of termination, or for Introductions made prior to termination that convert into a contract within **twelve (12) months** of termination.

G. **INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY**

   1. **Background IP:** Each Party retains ownership of its pre-existing intellectual property. No license is granted except as necessary to perform the services described herein.

   2. **Marketing Materials:** Any marketing materials or technical artifacts provided by the Referrer remain the property of the Referrer. The Recipient is granted a limited, non-exclusive license to use such materials solely for the purpose of pursuing the specific Opportunity associated with the Introduction.

H. **NON-CIRCUMVENTION**

   1. The Recipient agrees not to bypass, avoid, or circumvent the Referrer by entering into business directly with a Prospective Client introduced by the Referrer (including any rejected leads within the period specified in Section B.3) with the intent of excluding the Referrer from their rightful Business Development Fee. 

   2. This clause applies during the term of this Agreement and for a period of **twelve (12) months** following the termination or expiration of this Agreement.

I. **INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR**

   1. The Referrer is an independent contractor. Nothing in this Agreement shall create a partnership, joint venture, agency, or employment relationship. The Referrer has no authority to bind the Recipient to any contract or obligation.

J. **CONFIDENTIALITY**

   1. Each Party agrees to keep confidential all sensitive business information, trade secrets, and client data disclosed by the other Party. 

K. **ANTI-BRIBERY AND CORRUPTION (MACC ACT)**

   1. The Parties acknowledge the provisions of the **Malaysian Anti-Corruption Commission Act 2009** (specifically Section 17A regarding Corporate Liability). 

   2. The Referrer warrants that it has not and will not offer, promise, give, or accept any gratification, bribe, or corrupt inducement to/from any person (including public officials or employees of the Prospective Client) to secure business for the Recipient. 

   3. Breach of this clause shall grant the Recipient the right to terminate this Agreement immediately with no further liability to pay any outstanding fees.

L. **LIMITATION OF LIABILITY**

   1. **Indirect Damages:** To the maximum extent permitted by law, neither Party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages (including loss of profits) arising out of these terms.

   2. **Aggregate Cap:** Each Party’s total liability for all claims related to these terms shall not exceed the total Business Development Fees paid or payable under the applicable Referral Registration, or a fixed sum of RM 10,000 if no fee is applicable.

   3. **Exclusions:** The caps in Section L.2 shall not apply to breaches of Confidentiality, infringement of IP, or gross negligence/willful misconduct.

M. **NON-SOLICITATION**

   1. Neither Party shall knowingly solicit for employment any employee of the other Party who was directly involved in the activities hereunder during the term and for twelve (12) months thereafter, without prior written consent.

N. **MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS**

   1. **Severability:** If any provision is held illegal or unenforceable, it shall be severed, and the remaining provisions shall continue in full force.

   2. **Survival:** Provisions regarding Confidentiality, Non-Circumvention, Liability, and Audit shall survive termination.

   3. **Notices:** All notices shall be in writing and delivered personally, by email, or by recognized courier to the address specified in the primary agreement.

O. **GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION**

   1. This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the **Laws of Malaysia**. 

   2. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Courts of Malaysia.

**SCHEDULE A: FEES & DETAILS**

1. **Default Commission Rate**

   The default Business Development Fee shall be **5%** of the Gross Revenue received from the Successful Contract. 

2. **Validity Period (The "Tail")**

   Commissions are payable on revenue generated from the Prospective Client for a period of **12 months** from the date of the first Successful Contract. Any revenue generated after this period generally does not attract a commission (unless otherwise agreed).
