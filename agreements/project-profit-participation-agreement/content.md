(Collectively referred to as the "Parties" and individually as a "Party").

**WHEREAS:**

a) The Parties have previously teamed together to jointly identify, pursue, and secure the project known as "${PROJECT_NAME}" (the "Project") awarded by ${END_CLIENT_NAME} (the "Client").
b) The Parties have successfully won the Project.
c) The Prime will act as the principal contractor and front the contract with the Client (the "Main Contract").
d) The Prime intends to subcontract portions of the Project to the Subcontractors, and the Subcontractors intend to accept such subcontracting arrangements.
e) The Parties wish to establish a profit-sharing mechanism for the remaining profit upon full completion of the Project, after deducting all project-related costs.

<br/>

NOW, THEREFORE, the Parties agree as follows:

1. **Agreement Structure.** This Project Profit Participation Agreement, together with the documents listed below (collectively, the "Agreement"), constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. By signing this Agreement, the Parties agrees to be bound by the terms of the following documents, which are attached as exhibits and incorporated by reference:

   a) The executed Subcontractor Agreement between the Prime and each respective Subcontractor;

   b) General Subcontracting Terms and Conditions attached hereto.

   c) All Statements of Work (SOWs) executed referencing this Agreement.

2. **Order of Precedence.** In the event of any inconsistency or conflict between the documents comprising this Agreement, the following order of precedence shall apply (from highest to lowest priority):

   a) The specific terms entered in an applicable SOW referencing this Agreement;

   b) The specific terms entered in Schedule 2 (Fixed Price Scopes) or Schedule 3 (Variable Price Rates);

   c) The specific terms entered in this Agreement;

   d) General Subcontracting Terms and Conditions;

   e) Any other exhibits or attachments.

3. **Subcontracting Mechanisms**

   a) **Fixed Price Scope.** Specific scopes of work assigned to the Subcontractors on a fixed-price basis shall be set out in **Schedule 2: Fixed Price Scopes**. This schedule shall state the exact deliverables, milestones, the fixed subcontract price, and identify the specific Subcontractor responsible for delivering each scope.

   b) **Variable Price Scope.** Scopes of work that cannot be predetermined on a fixed-price basis or that require manpower augmentation shall be subcontracted at variable prices. Such variable pricing shall be calculated based on the transparent and agreed capability rates for different resources and different Subcontractors as fully detailed in **Schedule 3: Variable Price Rates**, multiplied by the actual time expended by the respective Subcontractor's personnel.

   c) **Performance of Work.** Each Subcontractor agrees to perform its respective subcontracted work in accordance with the specifications, schedules, and quality standards required under the Main Contract, this Agreement, and any associate schedules representing subcontracted work.

4. **Financial Arrangements and Payments**

   a) **Paid-When-Paid Principle.** The Parties acknowledge and agree that the Prime's obligation to make any payments to the Subcontractors, whether for fixed price scopes, variable price scopes, or Profit Share, is strictly contingent upon the Prime's actual, cleared receipt of corresponding funds from the Client for such work or milestones. Each Subcontractor expressly assumes the risk of non-payment or delayed payment by the Client.

   b) **Invoicing.** The Subcontractors shall submit invoices to the Prime in accordance with the milestones or timeframes set out in the applicable schedules.

   c) **Separate Project Account.** The Prime shall establish and maintain a separate bank account or a distinct ledger accounting mechanism (the "Project Account") strictly for tracking all receivables and money inflows received from the Client regarding the Project.

   d) **Cost Deduction.** All legitimate project-related costs, including but not limited to the Prime's direct execution costs, amounts paid or payable to the Subcontractors for fixed or variable scopes, third-party vendor costs, tax liabilities directly arising from the Project, and other agreed overhead or expenses, shall be deducted directly from the Project Account. To ensure transparency, deductions of variable costs incurred by any Party must strictly adhere to the documented resource rates defined in **Schedule 3: Variable Price Rates**.

5. **Profit Sharing**

   a) **Definition of Remaining Profit.** "Remaining Profit" shall mean the total gross revenue actually received by the Prime from the Client under the Main Contract for the Project, minus all legitimate, documented project-related costs deducted from the Project Account pursuant to Clause 4(d).

   b) **Profit Split.** Upon full completion of the Project, sign-off by the Client, and the final receipt of all payments owed by the Client under the Main Contract (including the release of any retention sums), the Prime shall calculate the Remaining Profit. The Remaining Profit shall be split uniformly among the Parties based on the percentages set forth in **Schedule 4: Profit Splitting Arrangement** (the "Profit Share").

   c) **Distribution of Profit Share.** The Prime shall transfer each Subcontractor's respective percentage of the Profit Share to them within thirty (30) days of the final determination and reconciliation of the Remaining Profit.

   d) **Audit Rights.** Each Subcontractor shall have the right, at its own expense and upon reasonable written notice, to audit the Project Account and relevant financial records of the Prime relating solely to the Project, to verify the accuracy of the Remaining Profit calculation and cost deductions.

6. **Term and Termination**

   a) **Term.** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall remain in full force and effect until the final reconciliation and payment of the Profit Share following the full completion of the Project.

   b) **Termination.** This Agreement may be terminated in the event of a material breach by either Party that remains uncured for thirty (30) days following written notice. In the event the Main Contract is terminated by the Client, this Agreement shall automatically terminate, and any accrued rights to payments (subject to the Paid-When-Paid Principle) or Profit Share up to the termination date shall be reconciled accordingly.

7. **General Provisions**

   a) **Confidentiality.** Both Parties agree to keep confidential any proprietary information received from the other Party or the Client in connection with the Project.

   b). **Independent Contractor.** Nothing contained in this Agreement shall be deemed to constitute, create, or otherwise recognize a joint venture, partnership, or formal business entity of any kind. The Parties are independent contractors, and neither Party has the authority to bind the other or incur any obligation on the other's behalf.

   c) **Governing Law and Jurisdiction.** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the jurisdiction where the Prime is registered, and the Parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts therein.
